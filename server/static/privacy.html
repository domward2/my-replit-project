Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how PnL AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and website (the "Service").

1) Who we are

Controller: PnL AI
Contact: support@pnlai.com
Address: Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Canary Islands, Spain

2) What we collect

  • Account data: email, name (if provided), and authentication identifiers.
  • OAuth tokens (Coinbase): access and refresh tokens and granted scopes, used to retrieve balances/transactions from your account. We do not receive your Coinbase password.
  • Exchange data: balances, assets, transactions/positions retrieved via OAuth/API for display in the app.
  • Usage data: logs, device/browser info, crash reports, and analytics (aggregate) to improve the Service.

3) What we do not do

  • No custody of your funds. Trades execute on your connected exchanges.
  • No sale of personal data. We do not sell or rent your information.
  • No withdrawal permissions requested from exchanges.

4) How we use information

  • To provide core features (portfolio display, activity history, signals).
  • To secure and operate the Service (auth, fraud prevention, debugging).
  • To communicate updates and important notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5) Legal bases (GDPR/EU)

  • Contract necessity: to deliver the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: security, service improvement, and fraud prevention.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., marketing emails).

6) Sharing

We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the Service (e.g., hosting, error monitoring). Providers are bound by confidentiality and data processing agreements. We may share data if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

7) Storage & security

  • OAuth tokens are stored server-side and protected (encryption at rest, access controls).
  • Transport is secured with HTTPS/TLS.
  • We regularly review access and rotate secrets.

8) Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal duties. You can request deletion at any time (see Your Rights).

9) Your rights

Subject to law, you may access, correct, delete, or port your data, and object/restrict certain processing. Contact support@pnlai.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10) International transfers

Where data is processed outside the EEA/UK, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) consistent with applicable law.

11) Third parties

When you connect Coinbase or other exchanges, we retrieve data via their APIs subject to those platforms' privacy terms. Please review Coinbase's policies as well.

12) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13) Changes

We may update this Policy. We will post the new version with a new effective date and notify you if changes are material.
