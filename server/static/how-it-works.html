How It Works

  1. Connect your exchange: Securely link your Coinbase, Kraken, Binance, or other supported exchange accounts via API keys or OAuth.
  2. Analyze sentiment: Our AI scans news, social media, and market data to generate real-time sentiment scores for top cryptocurrencies.
  3. Automate strategies: Set your trade logic once, then let our platform execute according to your chosen rules and risk limits.
  4. Track performance: View detailed PnL reports, asset allocations, and historical trades in a clean, intuitive dashboard.

We never store your funds. Your assets remain in your connected exchange accounts at all times.
