<h1>About Us</h1>
<p><strong>PnL-Ai</strong> is a Canary Islands–based technology startup developing next-generation crypto trading tools. Our platform helps traders connect their exchange accounts, analyze market sentiment in real-time, and automate strategies with minimal setup.</p>

<p>Founded in 2025, our mission is to make sophisticated crypto trading tools accessible to everyday investors — without requiring advanced coding skills or complex setups. We combine sentiment analysis, portfolio tracking, and smart automation into a single, easy-to-use interface.</p>

<h2>Our Team</h2>
<p>PnL-Ai was founded by a team of fintech and technology enthusiasts with deep experience in blockchain integration, data analytics, and automated trading systems.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Domain expertise:</strong> Crypto trading, sentiment analysis, and automated strategy deployment.</li>
  <li><strong>Technical skills:</strong> API integrations with major exchanges, scalable cloud architectures, and secure data handling.</li>
  <li><strong>Vision:</strong> Level the playing field in crypto markets by giving traders the same quality of tools used by professionals.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Where we operate</h2>
<p>Based in the Canary Islands, Spain, we operate within EU data protection and regulatory frameworks, ensuring user privacy and compliance.</p>

<h1>Our Mission</h1>
<p>Our mission at <strong>PnL-Ai</strong> is to empower traders with the tools they need to make better, faster, and more informed trading decisions — without needing to code or manually manage multiple exchange accounts.</p>

<h1>Our Vision</h1>
<p>We envision a future where traders of all levels can compete on equal footing, armed with the same AI-driven analysis, risk controls, and automation capabilities as institutional players.</p>

<h2>Core values</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Transparency:</strong> We never hide how our systems operate or the permissions they require.</li>
  <li><strong>Security:</strong> We design every integration with safety-first principles and no custody of user funds.</li>
